1. User creates project → Becomes Owner
   ↓
2. Owner sets project to OPEN → Accepting members
   ↓
3. Users request to join → JoinRequest created
   ↓
4. Owner/Lead approves/denies requests
   ↓
5. Users become Members → Can collaborate
   ↓
6. Lead assigns tasks to members
   ↓
7. Members work on tasks → Mark complete
   ↓
8. Lead reviews tasks → Approve/Request changes
   ↓
9. Project progresses → Milestones achieved
   ↓
10. Project completed → Mark as COMPLETED
